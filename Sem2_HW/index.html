Are you a cat person ?

Personally, I've never considered myself a cat person. I had two dogs growing up - will always love them, even though they are not around anymore, sadly.

But, what about about those hot-tempered felines ?

It's not that I ever dis-liked them, it turns out, I never made a proper cat friend before.

Until year 2019, when I met Lamar, who probably is the best kitty out there.
